FAQs

What is First Pick?

First Pick is an online marketplace which connects employers with the best recruiters, making it easy to source and compare quotes from our network of specialised headhunters. We ensure you only receive proposals from the most suitable recruiters for your search.

How much does it cost to get quotes?

Receiving quotes from our recruiters is free. We'll take your requirements, invite the best headhunters to quote a fixed fee for your search, and answer any questions you may have. If you decide to engage with your chosen recruiter and their search is successful, you'll be charged the quoted fee. We'll handle invoicing and have standard payment terms to keep things simple.

How long does it take to get quotes?

Once we have enough information from you and have invited the most suitable recruiters to quote, you'll typically receive 3-4 quotes within 24 hours.

Who will see my job details?

Our team will only share your job details with a select number (4-6) of the most suitable recruiters (right expertise, experience, and availability) to quote for your work. Your contact details are only shared with a recruiter if you've asked us to do so.
