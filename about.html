Rebuilding the recruitment sector

If you're an internal or external recruiter trying to find the best partners to work with, we're building First Pick for you.

What is First Pick?

First Pick matches employers with the world's best recruiters, making it easy to find the right headhunter for your search. Read more on how it works here.

Why we're building First Pick

At First Pick, we use technology to help employers find great external recruiters who offer high-end expertise on a low-overhead basis.

We believe there is a better way for in-house recruitment teams to source, compare, and hire specialist external headhunters. Trialling new recruitment agencies can be time consuming - First Pick's solution is easier, more cost-effective, and transparent.

We're a team of ex-headhunters who know the recruitment industry offers a valuable service. That's why we're building tools to help both external and internal recruiters work more efficiently. At First Pick, we're determined to bring the recruitment sector into the 21st Century.
