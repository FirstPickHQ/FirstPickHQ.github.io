Rebuilding the recruitment sector

What is First Pick?

First Pick connects employers with a network of professional headhunters who offer high-end expertise on a low-overhead basis. Read more on how it works here.

Why we're building First Pick

When we were working for search firms, we experienced the inefficiencies of the recruitment sector first hand. Employers are frustrated with high fees and questionable sales tactics, whilst recruiters are tired of being pitted against each other on contingent searches.

The current system doesn't work for either side, so we're building the technology to change the incentives. Recruitment is about relationships, and we want our headhunters to be able to focus on just that.

We also believe that the recruitment industry could and should do more to tackle the gender pay gap and diversity issues in certain sectors, and First Pick intends to give it the tools to do so.

Matthew Callery - Founder

Before founding First Pick, Matthew worked for 3 years as a headhunter at a start-up recruitment company in London.

After witnessing the industry's problems, he left to complete an MSc Computer Science at Bristol University, building CV Engineer along the way.

Matthew also holds an MA from the University of Cambridge.
