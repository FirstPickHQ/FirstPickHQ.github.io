Mission

Who we are

First Pick is a client-centric platform providing executive search services for fast-growing companies.

We are empowering our top headhunters by building machine learning software to map the executive landscape and automate repeatable processes, allowing them to spend more time advising and solving problems for our clients.

Join Our Team

Technical Founder

First Pick was founded by Matthew Callery, who holds an MSc Computer Science from the University of Bristol and a BA Materials Science from the University of Cambridge.

Matthew established the firm to solve the problems with executive search that he saw while making senior technical hires for financial services and FinTech clients in London. He's also probably the only exec search firm CEO with an active GitHub account, having built CV Engineer, a recruitment app with over 18,000 monthly active users.
