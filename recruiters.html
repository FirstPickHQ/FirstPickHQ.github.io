What is it like to be a headhunter on First Pick?

1

Browse new jobs

Review roles from employers who are actively looking to engage a headhunter.

2

Submit your quote

Submit a quote setting out what you can offer the employer, including the fixed fee you would charge to fill the role.

3

Start the search

If the client selects your quote, you will receive their contact details and can begin the search.

4

Get paid

If you successfully fill the vacancy, the client pays the fixed fee agreed in your quote.
