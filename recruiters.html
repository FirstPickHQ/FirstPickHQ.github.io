What is it like to be a headhunter on First Pick?

1. Browse new jobs

Review roles from employers who are actively looking to engage a headhunter.

2. Submit your quote

If you like the role and employer, you can submit a quote setting out what you can offer them, including the fixed fee you would charge to fill the role.

3. Start the search

If the client selects your quote, you will receive their contact details and can begin the search.

4. Get paid

If you successfully fill the vacancy, the client pays the fixed fee agreed in your quote. First Pick is free to join, there are no membership fees, and it is free to submit quotes. We handle the invoices, and our service fee is a percentage of the fee the client pays you.

Mission

We believe that the recruitment sector is unfairly vilified due to some bad actors in the profession. We're working to restore trust in the industry with your help.
