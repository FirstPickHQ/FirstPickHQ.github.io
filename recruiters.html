FAQs

What is First Pick?

First Pick is an online marketplace which connects employers with the best headhunters. We offer you access to new clients and each search is on an exclusive basis. Our aim is to build a platform which benefits both headhunters and employers.

How and when will I get paid?

To simplify payments for employers using First Pick, we act as a master vendor. We invoice the employer on the candidate's start date and our payment terms are 30 days. You'll get paid the day we get paid. In the future, we plan to pay you on the candidate's start date to benefit your cash flow.

How do rebates work?

First, we both cry. Then we'll assess the damage. If the candidate leaves between 0-30 days, the employer gets a 100% rebate and you'll be required to return your full fee to us (if it has been paid out). If the candidate leaves between 30-60 days, the employer gets a 50% rebate and you'll be required to return half your fee to us. In the future, we plan to insure headhunters against rebates (we know they're a cash flow nightmare!).

What if an employer doesn't pay my placement fee?

We have clear payment terms and a rigorous collections process. If the payment has not been received within 30 days of the candidate starting, we will notify you and keep you updated with our progress. Ultimately, we do have the right to assign debt collection over to you, but this is highly unlikely to happen. In the future, we plan to insure headhunters against this scenario so that you always get paid on time.

There's a better way for recruitment to work

We believe the recruitment sector is unfairly vilified due to some bad actors in the profession. We're working to restore trust in the industry with your help.
